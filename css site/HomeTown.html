My Home Town is Karimnagar

Karimnagar is a historic city located in the state of Telangana, India. It is known for its rich cultural heritage, vibrant traditions and beautiful architecture.

History of Karimnagar

According to historical records, Karimnagar was founded in the 18th century by Syed Karimullah Shah Saheb Quadri, a prominent Sufi saint. The city has witnessed many significant events throughout history, including the rule of the Kakatiya dynasty, the Bahmani Sultanate and the Nizam of Hyderabad.

Best Places to Visit in Karimnagar

  • Elgandal Fort
  • Kaleshwaram Temple
  • Manair Dam
  • Lower Manair Dam
  • Vemulawada Temple

Elgandal Fort

  • Elgandal Fort

    • The Elgandal Fort is one of the most prominent historical landmarks in Karimnagar. It was built during the Kakatiya dynasty and served as the capital of the region for many years. The fort is located on a hill and offers panoramic views of the surrounding area.

    Kaleshwaram Temple

  • Kaleshwaram Temple

    • The Kaleshwaram Temple is a popular pilgrimage site in Karimnagar. It is dedicated to Lord Shiva and is believed to be one of the oldest temples in the region. The temple is known for its stunning architecture and is surrounded by beautiful natural scenery.

    Lower Manair Dam

  • Lower Manair Dam

    • The Manair Dam is a major tourist attraction in Karimnagar. It was built on the Manair River and is known for its beautiful gardens and scenic views. The dam is also a popular spot for boating and fishing.

    Mid Manair Dam

  • Mid Manair Dam

    • The Mid Manair Dam is another popular tourist attraction in Karimnagar. It was built on the Lower Manair River and is known for its beautiful sunsets and scenic views. The dam is also a popular spot for picnics and family outings.

    Vemulawada Temple

  • Vemulawada Temple

    • The Vemulawada Temple is a famous pilgrimage site in Karimnagar. It is dedicated to Lord Raja Rajeshwara Swamy and is believed to be one of the most powerful temples in the region. The temple is known for its beautiful architecture and is surrounded by lush greenery.
